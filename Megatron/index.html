MEGATRON

Megatron, the feared Decepticon leader, believes Transformers are destined to conquer the universe, opposing those who seek peace. Driven by a mix of grand ambition and personal power, he uses bold, unsubtle tactics and retreats when needed. Though he seldom defeats Optimus Prime, Megatron excels at finding energy sources and quickly deals with those who challenge him, making death by his hand an "honor."
